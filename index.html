Alan Shearer

An English football hero.

Alan Shearer

    Here's a time line of Shearers career:

  • 1970 - Bornin Gosforth, Newcastle.
  • 1988 - Makes his debut as substitute in a First division fixture against Chelsea after being promoted from Southampton FC youth team.
  • 1988 - Scores his first hat-trick in his first full debut helping his team beat Arsenal 4 : 2.
  • 1992 - Made his debut in the English national team, scoring a goal in a 2 : 0 win against France.
  • 1992 - Joins Blackburn Rovers for English transfer record-breaking £3.6 million fee.
  • 1995 - Wins the Premier League with Blackburn Rovers scoring 34 goals in a season.
  • 1996 - Joins his hometown club Newcastle United for a world transfer record-breaking £15 million fee.
  • 1999 - Scores his only hat-trick for England in a 6 : 0 win against Luxembourg.
  • 2003 - Champions League hat-trick against Bayern Leverkusen.
  • 2006 - Retires from proffessional football having scored 313 goals in 622 appearences for club and country.
